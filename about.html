About Further Compare

Further Compare is a modern, professional web-based tool for file and text comparison, built for enterprise-grade reliability and security. Our platform brings powerful, user-friendly visualization and merge features to your everyday work.

Features
  • Side-by-side comparison of two files or text blocks
  • Inline color-coded difference highlighting
  • Copy individual or all differences from one side to another with a single click
  • Support for whitespace/tab-ignore mode for minor changes
  • Upload, edit, and compare instantly – all operations happen locally in your browser
  • Responsive design, intuitive UI, and dark-mode readiness
  • No data leaves your device; privacy by design
Our Mission

FileDiff Enterprise is dedicated to empowering teams to review, merge, and audit their source files and documents with speed, accuracy, and total data privacy.
